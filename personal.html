Personal Information

Beyoncé was born Beyoncé Giselle Knowles on September 4, 1981 in Houston, Texas, U.S.A.
She has lived in Bel Air, Los Angeles since 2017. She and her husband, Jay-Z, bought their newly-built 30,000-square-foot mansion for $88 million. The mansion includes six separate structures and covers 1.5 acres of land.

Husband

Beyoncé has been married to Jay-Z since April 4, 2008.













Oldest Daughter

Blue Ivy Carter was born on January 7, 2012, at Lenox Hill hospital in New York.













Twins

On June 13, 2017, Beyonce gave birth to twins, Rumi and Sir Carter. The twins were delivered by Dr Paul Crane at Ronald Reagan UCLA Medical Center in Los Angeles.
